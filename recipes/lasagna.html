Lasagna

Description

Lasagna consists of alternate layers of pasta sheets and sauce

The sauce can be either meat based or vegetarian. Traditionally the sauce is a bolognase sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Minced Meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Make Red Sauce
  2. Make Cheese Sauce
  3. In baking pan, layer Pasta sheets, Red sauce and cheese sauce
  4. Bake in oven for 45 minutes at 220 deg Celsius
